Slow Cooker Cowboy Beans

Description

Cowboy beans (a.k.a. chuckwagon) is a southwestern dish consisting of beans, ground beef, bacon, and other ingredients. Full of barbecue flavor, this side pairs perfectly with all kinds of entrees.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • Â½ pound bacon, chopped
  • 1 (28 ounce) can baked beans with pork
  • 1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 (15 ounce) can lima beans, rinsed and drained
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon prepared mustard

Steps

  1. Gather all your ingredients.
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir ground beef and bacon together in the hot skillet until beef is browned and crumbly, about 7 to 10 minutes.
  3. Transfer hamburger and bacon into a slow cooker; stir in baked beans with pork, kidney beans, lima beans, onions, ketchup, brown sugar, and mustard.
  4. Cover and cook on Low until thickened and bubbling, at least 3 hours.
